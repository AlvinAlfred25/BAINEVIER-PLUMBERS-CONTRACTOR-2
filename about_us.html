About Bainevier

Who We Are

A consultancy-first plumbing and tiling firm built on technical planning, not guesswork.

Engineering-Minded Plumbing

Bainevier Plumbers & Contractors is a professional consultancy firm dedicated to delivering high-quality plumbing advice and system planning services. We work closely with clients, engineers, architects, and contractors to ensure plumbing systems meet modern standards.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, innovative, and sustainable plumbing consultancy services that exceed our clients' expectations.

Our Vision

To become a leading plumbing consultancy firm recognized for technical excellence and integrity.

How We Work

Every system is designed and reviewed before installation begins, so problems get caught on paper, not in the wall.

Meet The Founder

Mwesigye Lawrence

Founder, Bainevier Plumbers & Contractors

Mwesigye Lawrence founded Bainevier Plumbers & Contractors with a simple goal: bring proper engineering discipline to plumbing and tiling work that is too often done by guesswork. Under his direction, every project starts on paper — designed and reviewed — before any pipe is cut or tile is laid.

He oversees every job personally, working alongside clients, engineers, and architects to make sure each system is built to last.
